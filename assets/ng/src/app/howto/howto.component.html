How to? This page covers in depth mechanics of the game

The object of the Game is to reduce your Opponent's Life to 0

Gameplay Your turn will have several Phases, outlined here

Each Phase requires a RESPONSE from you!

This is because you may make a Play before an opponent has had a chance for their Play to fully complete



Phases
      Sunrise   the start of a turn
  • Start: Trigger Sunrise Token Powers
  • Any Player: Add an Element to Karma; Play Spell Cards; Trigger Token Powers
  • My Turn: Karma is re-activated; Draw a Card; in addition to above

      Main   the body of a turn
  • Any Player: Play Spell Cards; Trigger Token Powers
  • My Turn: Play Being or Item Cards; Attack with a Being Token; in addition to above

      Sunset   the end of a turn
  • Start: Trigger Sunset Token Powers
  • Any Player: Play Spell Cards; Trigger Token Powers

      Attack   being declared an attack
  • Any Player: Play Spell Cards; Trigger Token Powers
  • Not My Turn: Defend with a Being Token; in addition to above

      Combat   defendant declared how it defends
  • Any Player: Play Spell Cards; Trigger Token Powers
  • End: Attacking Token deals Damage (if alive); Defending Token loses Health if alive, else Opponent loses Life

      Trigger   a play has been made or a power triggered
  • Any Player: Play Spell Cards; Trigger Token Powers

      Target   selecting a target
  • Any Player: Play Spell Cards; Trigger Token Powers
  • My Turn: Declare a Target


Making a Play Cards have a cost to Play, and this is paid from your in-game Karma

You may only play Cards which you have enough Elements to use at the time

The title panel on each Card displays it's Karmic Cost

The second panel on each card, below its' photo, displays it's Type, keep reading for more information about Cards


Karma The kinds of Plays that you make are determined by the color of Card that your playing, of these 7 element colors



Each card played deactivates elements of your Karma

Each turn, you chooes an Element to add to your Karma

Elements of your Karma will reactivate again at the start of your turn


Cards Your cards start out in your Future, are drawn into your Hand, and tend towards entering your Past

Cards are of 3 kinds: Beings, Items, and Spells

    Playing a Card
  • Requires the Game to be in the appropriate Phase
  • Requires your Karma to contain the Elements printed the Card
  • Causes Elements of your Karma to deactivate
  • Cause the Game to enter State Trigger


Playing Being and Item Cards will create Tokens in the Present for your Seat in the Game


Powers Being and Item Tokens, or Spell Plays, may have any number and variety of Powers

Some Powers are Targeted, meaning they require a Target, as in
Target Being   Target Player or Being   Target Being or Item

They may require additional Elements to be paid from your Karma

When a Token has a Power, it requires the Token to be Awake, and causes the Token to become Asleep
