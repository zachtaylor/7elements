Objective


Each player begins the game with 7 Life. When a players' Life reaches 0, that players loses. When a player has no more opponents, they've won!


You begin the game with a Deck of cards, and these cards can be used to push and pull the game in your favor


There are different types of cards


You can play spells, or summon helpful items and beings


Items can be used every turn, after you have summoned them


Beings may have a variety of additional powers, but can also help attack your opponent, and defend your own Life. Beings cannot be used the same turn they are summoned.


Spells may be played as a reaction to other events. This may be the summoning of a Being or Item, or another Spell!


There are different elements


All cards have associated element costs. These costs are paid from your active Karma


Each turn, before you draw a card, choose any element to add to your available Karma, and it will be active in the same turn, onward


Each card played deactivates elements of your Karma, but these elements become active again every turn, at Sunrise, on your turn
