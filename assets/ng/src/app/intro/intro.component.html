Welcome to 7 Elements Online

Introduction


This is a tabletop-style online card-based game. Players use Cards to defeat their Opponents.

In preparation for a game, Players must choose which Deck of Cards to play. Several free-to-play decks are available.

Players can collect Cards to build their own Decks, and win Coins by playing the game.

Rules
    For each Player
  • A Deck of Cards is shuffled, resulting order hidden, called Future
  • Draw Cards (format variable), called Hand
  • Set Starting Life (format variable)
    • Choice
    • Keep: Proceed
    • Mulligan: Put your Hand aside, called Past, Draw Cards (format variable)

The game proceeds by randomly selecting an Active Player

    Turn Phases
    • Sunrise
    • All Tokens become Awake
    • Active Players' Elements become Active
    • All Players may play Spell Cards and activate Abilities
    • All Players create a new Element (not Active)
    • Active Player draws a Card if their Future is not empty
    • Main
    • Active Player may play any type of Cards and activate Abilities
    • Active Player may use Awake Beings to Attack
    • Play
    • All Players may play Spell Cards and activate Abilities
    • Attack
    • All Players may play Spell Cards and activate Abilities
    • Defending Players may use Awake Beings to Combat
    • If no Defending Players use any Awake Beings to Combat, go to Combat
    • Combat
    • All Players may play Spell Cards and activate Abilities
    • If Defending Being is declared, Attacking Being and Defending Being deal Damage to each other
    • If Defending Being is not declared, Attacking Being deals Damage to Defending Player
    • Sunset
    • All Players may play Spell Cards and activate Abilities

At the conclusion of Sunset, the next Player becomes the Active Player at Sunrise

Reduce your Opponents' Life to 0 to win the game!


Being Cards These Cards add Being Tokens to the Present, and the Card is added to your Past

Being Tokens in the Present have their own Life, and when this reaches 0, the Being is removed from the Present

Being Tokens in the Present may be Awake or Asleep, and must be Awake to be used

Beings do not (re-)gain Life unless otherwise specified

Being Tokens are Asleep when they enter the Present
