Introduction You begin the game with a Deck of Cards, called your Future, and 7 Life

When a Players' Life reaches 0, that Player loses, and when a Player has no more Opponents, they win!


Starting the Game In order to play, you must be logged in, and then you may Click here to start playing

When the Game starts, you Future will be Shuffled, and you Draw 3 Cards

Your first choice is whether to keep your starting Hand, or Mulligan

You may keep your starting hand, by clicking "Keep"

Or, you may click "Mulligan", which will put all 3 Cards from your Hand into your Past, and you will then Draw 3 Cards


Being Cards These Cards add Being Tokens to the Present, and the Card is added to your Past

Being Tokens in the Present have their own Health, and when this reaches 0, the Being is removed from the Present

Being Tokens in the Present may be Awake or Asleep, and must be Awake to be used

Beings do not (re-)gain Health unless otherwise specified

Being Tokens are Asleep when they enter the Present
