v0.0.2 2020-01-12
    • General
    • This version contains known and unknown issues
    • I will apply fixes as I identify these, and you can let me know of any issues found by visiting the project on Github
    • Account Support
    • All accounts have been gifted with 21 coins
    • Added support for email change
    • Added support for password change
    • Deck Builder
    • Added initial support
    • Play Mode
      • Gameplay
      • Playing Being Cards will now create a Being Token, and playing Item Cards will now create Item Tokens, in the Present
      • Each Sunrise will allow every Player to create an Element, and Trigger Sunrise on every Token
      • Each Sunset will Trigger Sunset on every Token
      • Free to Play Decks
      • removed previous Free to Play Decks
      • AllCards: 1 copy of each Card
      • WhiteGold: 3 of each Time Runner, Zealot, New Element, Burn, Energize, Banhammer, Call the Banners
      • GreenGold: 3 of each Zealot, Vine Spirit, Energize, Inspire Growth, Grace, Call the Banners, Symbiosis
      • RedBlue: Ifrit, Water Dancer, Nightmare Ader, Burn, Wormhole, Burning Rage, Crystal Ball
      • ItemTech: Time Runner, New Element, Burn, Banhammer, Burning Rage, Symbiosis, Crystal Ball
      • Sk8_H1: Pixie, Nightmare Ader, Grace, Memorialize, Symbiosis, Font of Life, Necromancy 101
      • KetchupMustard: Ifrit, Zealot, Burn, Energize, Memorialize, Burning Rage, Call the Banners
      • VS AI
      • Added AI ability targeting
      • Added AI decision prioritization
    • Cards Added
      • Banhammer
      • Cost:
      • Type: Item
      • : Remove Target Being or Item from the Past
      • Burning Rage
      • Cost:
      • Type: Item
      • : Burning Rage does 1 Damage to each enemy Player
      • Call the Banners
      • Cost:
      • Type: Spell
      • Add 3 Beings to your Present, each with 2 Attack and 2 Health
      • Symbiosis
      • Cost:
      • Type: Item
      • : Target Being gets 1 Attack
      • Crystal Ball
      • Cost:
      • Type: Item
      • : Look at the next Card in your Future, then you can choose to Shuffle your Future
      • Font of Life
      • Cost:
      • Type: Item
      • : You gain 2 Life
      • : Target Being gains 2 Health
      • Necromancy 101
      • Cost:
      • Type: Item
      • : Add Target Being from your Past to your Present, set its' Health to 1, you lose 1 Life
    • Cards Changed
      • Time Runner
      • Type: Being 0 / 2
      • : Draw a Card
      • added karmic cost to ability, added 1 health to compensate
      • Ifrit
      • Type: Being 2 / 1
      • : Deal 1 Damage to Target Being or Player
      • added turn requirement to ability, added 1 attack to compensate
      • Vine Spirit
      • Type: Being 0 / 2
      • reduced attack, add health to respond to the change to Sunrise Triggers
      • Pixie
      • Type: Being 1 / 2
      • add health to compensate buff to aggro strategies
      • Nightmare Ader
      • Type: Being 0 / 3
      • add health to compensate buff to aggro strategies
      • Wormhole
      • Target Being's Owner adds a Copy of it to their Hand, remove it from the Present
      • updated behavior for game tokens
      • Grace
      • Target Being gains 3 Health
      • add health to compensate buff to aggro strategies
